**Role:** Innkeeper of the Broken Wheel Tavern, Millhaven  
**Status:** Allied — offered the party their first job  
**First met:** [Session 01](/sessions/session-01/)

## Description

A stout woman in her fifties with callused hands and a sharp eye. She runs the Broken Wheel with no-nonsense efficiency and obvious pride. She's lived in Millhaven her whole life and knows everyone's business.

## Personality

Practical and direct. She doesn't trust easily but respects competence. She worries about the disappearances more than she lets on — one of the missing is her nephew.

## What She Knows

- Three people have vanished near the old mill in the past two weeks
- The Constable is brushing it off as animal attacks
- Someone has been buying large quantities of rope and lantern oil from the general store

## Hooks

- Her nephew Piotr is one of the missing
- She owes a favour to someone in the capital — she won't say who
