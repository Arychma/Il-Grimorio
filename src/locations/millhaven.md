**Type:** Small town, farming community  
**Region:** The Reaches  
**Population:** ~400  
**First visited:** [Session 01](/sessions/session-01/)

## Description

A quiet agricultural town at the edge of the Reaches, known for its grain mill and little else. The houses are timber-framed and low, clustered around a central well. The streets smell of hay and woodsmoke.

It's the kind of place that expects nothing to happen — and is usually right.

## Points of Interest

- **The Broken Wheel Tavern** — The only inn in town. Run by [Greta Stobbe](/npcs/greta-stobbe/).
- **The Old Mill** — On the edge of town, partially ruined. Recently associated with disappearances.
- **Constable's Office** — A single room attached to the general store.

## Current Situation

Three townsfolk have gone missing near the old mill. The Constable claims animal attacks. The party has been hired to investigate.

## Atmosphere

> Millhaven is the kind of place you pass through, not the kind you choose. The people are polite the way people are polite when they don't trust strangers — which is to say, very.
