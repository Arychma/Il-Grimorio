## What Happened

The party arrived separately in the town of **Millhaven**, each drawn by different threads — a rumor of work, a debt to collect, a name on a wanted poster.

They met at the *Broken Wheel Tavern*, where the innkeeper **Greta Stobbe** was offering coin for anyone willing to look into disappearances near the old mill on the edge of town.

> "Three people gone in a fortnight. The watch says wolves. I say wolves don't leave boots behind." — Greta Stobbe

## Key Moments

- Theron picked the lock on the grain store and found a hidden trapdoor
- Maela failed her History check on the old mill ruins (badly)
- The party decided NOT to go in at night — a rare moment of wisdom

## NPCs Met

- [Greta Stobbe](/npcs/greta-stobbe/) — Innkeeper, worried, practical
- [Constable Renn](/npcs/constable-renn/) — Skeptical of the party, hiding something
